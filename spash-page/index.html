What should we do with the 980 freeway?

The sky is the limit

A project by The California Department of Transportation (Caltrans)

Please join our mailing list to stay informed!

The I-980 freeway is a barrier to travel and economic opportunities between Downtown and the historically Black neighborhood of West Oakland. Acknowledging past injustices to underserved communities and seeking to repair harm, Caltrans needs your help to reimagine the possibilities for the I-980 corridor.

Project Contacts: Randolph Belle, Community Visioning Coordinator, RBA Creative - randolph@rbacreative.com and Becky Frank, Community Planning Branch Chief, Caltrans Bay Area - Vision980@dot.ca.gov
